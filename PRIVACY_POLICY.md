# Privacy Policy — Knight Tour Trainer

**Effective date:** 2026-01-03

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Knight Tour Trainer** app (“the App”) collects and uses information.

## Summary
- The App uses **Firebase Crashlytics** to collect crash reports.
- The App uses **Firebase Analytics** to collect basic usage analytics.
- The App does **not** request or access **precise location** (GPS).
- The current version does **not** show ads.

## Information the App may collect

### 1) Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics)
Crash reports may include:
- device information (model, OS version)
- app version
- crash logs / stack traces
- basic diagnostics related to the crash

Purpose: to detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and stability issues.

### 2) Usage analytics (Firebase Analytics)
Analytics data may include:
- events related to app usage (e.g., screen views, feature usage)
- device and app information (app version, OS version, device type)
- approximate location derived from network information (e.g., city/region), such as IP-based estimation

Purpose: to understand which features are used and improve the App.

> The App does not request or access precise GPS location.

## Advertising
The current version of the App does **not** display ads.
Future versions may include advertising. If advertising is added, this policy will be updated before release.

## Data sharing
The App shares data with **Google Firebase** (Crashlytics and Analytics) as a service provider to operate these features.

## Data retention
Data retention is managed by Firebase and may depend on your device settings and Firebase configuration.

## Your choices
Depending on your device settings and region, you may be able to limit analytics collection through system privacy controls. You can also stop using the App at any time.
